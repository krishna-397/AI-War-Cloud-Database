# Talks, Lecture-Performances & Podcasts by Sarah Ciston on AI War Cloud
Type: Reference list of author's spoken material (abstracts author-written). Fetched links verified 2026-07-01.

## "Inside the AI War Cloud" — lecture-performance, Ars Electronica Festival 2025 (theme: Panic), Linz, Sept 3–7 2025
https://ars.electronica.art/panic/en/view/inside-the-ai-war-cloud-22638ddb450c81db89adde611f37652a/
Dives into examples from the database, its organizing themes, and how it was built using critical code studies, open-source intelligence, and artistic research. Deconstructs "AI decision support" as a misdirect from problems common to all AI systems, which "amplify, accelerate, and neutral-wash biased human processes."

## "Mapping the AI War Cloud" — re:publica 25, Berlin
https://re-publica.com/en/node/10597
Explores intersections between commercial Big Tech and the military-industrial complex; traces the "techno-imperial boomerang" from consumer market to battlefield and back. (Video recording on re:publica YouTube channel.)
